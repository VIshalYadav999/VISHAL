About Our Company

Pioneers in Industrial Manufacturing

Founded in 1995, Prime Industrial Solutions has grown from a small machine shop to a full-service industrial manufacturer serving clients across North America.

Our 25,000 square foot facility houses state-of-the-art CNC machines, precision measuring equipment, and a team of highly skilled technicians.

We pride ourselves on quality craftsmanship, on-time delivery, and competitive pricing for all our industrial components and services.
