About JDS

Who we are and what we stand for

Our Mission to Serve

Founded in 2010, JDS is a nonprofit organization dedicated to alleviating poverty through two key initiatives: feeding the hungry and supporting underprivileged couples with marriage assistance.

What began as a small community effort has grown into a movement that serves thousands of people annually across multiple districts. Our values of compassion, integrity, and service guide all our actions.

We believe everyone deserves basic human dignity, which starts with access to food and the ability to celebrate life's important milestones like marriage.

Our Services

How we're making a difference

Food Distribution

We organize regular food drives and community kitchens to ensure no one in our community goes hungry. Our programs serve over 1,500 meals each week.

Learn More

Marriage Assistance

Our marriage support program helps underprivileged couples with financial aid, counseling, and wedding arrangements. Last year we assisted 85 weddings.

Learn More

Community Education

We conduct workshops on nutrition, financial literacy, and family values to create lasting change in our communities.
