Who We Are

JDS Donation Committee is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving lives through charitable giving and community development initiatives. Founded in 2010, we've grown from a local initiative to a national movement.

Our work focuses on education, healthcare access, and sustainable community projects that create long-term impact. We believe in transparency, accountability, and the power of collective action.

  • Over 150 projects completed since inception
  • Direct impact on 50,000+ lives annually
  • 93% of donations go directly to programs
