About Us

Our Company

Precision Mould Solutions is a leading manufacturer of custom injection molds and tooling. Founded in 2000, we've earned a reputation for excellence in mold design, engineering, and manufacturing.

Our team of skilled engineers and technicians brings decades of combined experience to every project, ensuring the highest quality molds that deliver consistent performance and longevity.

We specialize in complex, high-precision molds for industries including automotive, medical, consumer products, and industrial applications.
